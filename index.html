How to Play Sequence

The Goal

Guess the number that completes the sequence using one of the six options below. You have three chances.

The Interwoven Rule

The sequence is **interwoven**. Find the pattern by looking at the **1st, 3rd, and the missing (5th) number**. The 2nd and 4th numbers form a separate distractor sequence.

Color Feedback

  • GREEN: Correctly solved the main (1st, 3rd, 5th) interwoven rule.
  • GOLD (Salmon): Correct number, but you used the wrong (distractor) sequence rule, or an unexpected simple rule.
  • GREY: Guess was incorrect.
