How to Play Sequence

The Goal

Your goal is to find the number that logically completes a three-number sequence.

Step 1: Find the Distractor

Of the four numbers shown, three form a sequence and one is a distractor. Click on the number you believe is the distractor to remove it.

Step 2: Solve the Sequence

Once you've removed the distractor, analyze the remaining three numbers to find the pattern. Then, select the correct answer from the options below.
