How to Play Sequence

The Goal

Guess the number that completes the sequence using one of the six options below. You have three chances.

The Interwoven Rule

The sequence is interwoven. Find the pattern by looking at the 1st, 3rd, and missing (5th) number. The 2nd and 4th form a separate distractor sequence.
